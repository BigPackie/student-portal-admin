The sizes of the images are sufficient to keep them detailed enough on common size mobile devices.
Common size mobile device has a size of 6.39 inches, with screen resolution 1080 x 2340 pixels and 19.5:9 aspect ration.

It's possible to make them with higher or lower resolution too, but the aspect ratio of the image should not be changed.
